# Content Marketing Templates

## 1. Blog Post Templates

### 1.1 How-To Article Template

```
Blog Post: How-To Article
============================

Title: How to [Achieve Desired Outcome] in [Timeframe/Context]

Meta Description: [150-160 characters summarizing the article]

Introduction (150-200 words):
- Hook: [Start with a problem or statistic]
- Problem: [Explain the challenge]
- Solution: [Introduce your approach]
- Promise: [What readers will learn]
- CTA: [What they'll achieve by reading]

Section 1: [Main Concept] (300-400 words)
- [Explain the concept]
- [Include examples]
- [Add visual or screenshot if applicable]

Section 2: [Step 1] (200-300 words)
- [Clear instruction]
- [Why this step matters]
- [Common mistakes to avoid]

Section 3: [Step 2] (200-300 words)
- [Clear instruction]
- [Why this step matters]
- [Common mistakes to avoid]

Section 4: [Step 3] (200-300 words)
- [Clear instruction]
- [Why this step matters]
- [Common mistakes to avoid]

Section 5: [Advanced Tips] (Optional) (200-300 words)
- [Pro tips for readers ready for more]
- [Advanced techniques]

Conclusion (150-200 words):
- Recap: [3-5 key takeaways]
- Next Steps: [What to do next]
- CTA: [Related content or product]

Internal Links: [2-3 links to related content]
External Links: [3-5 authoritative sources]
```

### 1.2 Listicle Template

```
Blog Post: Listicicle
======================

Title: [Number] [Ways to Achieve X] / [Benefits of Y]

Meta Description: [150-160 characters]

Introduction (150-200 words):
- Hook: [Start with a compelling statistic or question]
- Setup: [Explain why this topic matters]
- Promise: [What readers will get]
- Preview: [Brief overview of the list]

List Items:

1. [First Item] (200-300 words each)
   - [Main point]
   - [Why it matters]
   - [Example or case study]
   - [How to implement]

2. [Second Item]
   - [Main point]
   - [Why it matters]
   - [Example or case study]
   - [How to implement]

3. [Third Item]
   - ...

[N through X items]

Conclusion (150-200 words):
- Recap: [Wrap up the main theme]
- Bonus Tip: [One additional insight]
- CTA: [Related content or product signup]
```

## 5. Case Study Framework

### 5.1 Structure

```
Case Study Structure
====================

1. Title
   [Company] + [Result] + [Timeframe]

2. Executive Summary (100-150 words)
   The challenge, solution, and results

3. Company Background (100-200 words)
   Industry, size, location, website

4. The Challenge (200-300 words)
   Specific problems they faced

5. The Solution (300-500 words)
   How your product/service solved it

6. The Results (300-500 words)
   Quantifiable outcomes with metrics

7. Testimonial Quote (50-100 words)

8. Key Takeaways (150-200 words)
   Lessons learned

9. About [Your Company] (50-100 words)
```

### 5.2 Metrics Format

```
Results Template
===============

Before → After: [Metric Name]
-------------------------
Before: [Number/State]
After: [Number/State]
Improvement: [% or X times]

Time to Results: [Duration]

ROI: [% or multiple]

Customer Quote:
"[Specific quote about the results]"
```

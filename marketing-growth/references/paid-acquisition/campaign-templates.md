```

Programmatic Display Campaign Ad Group Architecture

----------------------------------------

----------------------------------

- Industry-specific publications

- CPM bidding
- Brand safety priority
- Viewability guarantee
```

## 4. Campaign Launch Checklist

```
Campaign Launch Checklist
========================

PRE-LAUNCH (1-2 Weeks Before)
==============================
□ Campaign brief approved
□ Budget allocated and confirmed
□ Targeting parameters defined
□ Creative assets completed
□ Landing pages built and tested
□ Tracking implemented
□ UTM parameters configured

TECHNICAL SETUP
===============
□ Pixel/tracking tags installed
□ Conversion events configured
□ View-through windows set
□ Attribution model selected
□ Cross-device tracking enabled
□ Mobile app SDK integrated (if applicable)

CREATIVE REVIEW
===============
□ All ad copy proofread
□ Images meet platform specs
□ Video duration verified
□ Landing page URLs tested
□ CTA buttons functional
□ Brand guidelines followed

TARGETING VERIFICATION
=======================
□ Audience size within range
□ Age/gender distribution correct
□ Location targeting accurate
□ Interest targeting appropriate
□ Negative audiences configured
□ Frequency caps set

BUDGET & BIDDING
================
□ Daily/monthly budget set
□ Bidding strategy selected
□ Bid limits configured
□ Pacing strategy defined
□ Campaign spend limits set

COMPLIANCE & LEGAL
==================
□ Ad copy approved by legal
□ Privacy policy linked
□ Terms & conditions present
□ Disclaimer language included
□ Industry-specific requirements met

LAUNCH DAY
==========
□ Campaign status confirmed active
□ Initial pacing looks correct
□ Tracking data flowing
□ No technical errors
□ Team notified of launch

POST-LAUNCH (First 48 Hours)
============================
□ Performance metrics reviewed
□ Spend pacing on track
□ Impressions being served
□ CTR within expected range
□ No delivery issues
□ Optimization plan ready

POST-LAUNCH (Week 1)
====================
□ Full performance review
□ A/B test setup confirmed
□ Initial optimization decisions made
□ Reporting dashboard updated
□ Stakeholder update prepared
```

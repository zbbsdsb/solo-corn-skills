# Marketing Playbooks

## Product Hunt Launch

### 1. Playbook Overview

```
Playbook: Product Hunt Launch
=============================

Objective: Achieve top 5 product of the day on Product Hunt
Timeline: 4 weeks preparation + Launch Day + 1 week follow-up
Platform: Product Hunt (producthunt.com)
Success Metric: Top 5 Product of the Day, 300+ upvotes

Key Success Factors:
- Strong hunter with engaged audience
- Compelling product story and visuals
- Active community engagement before and during launch
- Well-timed launch for target timezone
- Prepared network for initial traction
```


### 2. Launch Checklist

#### Pre-Launch Preparation (Weeks 1-4)

```
WEEK 1: HUNTER & NETWORK SETUP
===============================

Day 1-2: Hunter Acquisition
---------------------------
Tasks:
□ Research potential hunters (500+ followers, active in your category)
□ Identify 10-15 potential hunters via PH search
□ Check hunter's recent posts for engagement quality
□ Verify hunter's audience demographics match your target
□ Prepare personalized outreach message

Hunter Outreach Template:
"Hi [Name],

I've been following your launches and noticed you often post about [category].
I recently built [product name] - [one sentence value prop].

Would you be interested in hunting it? Happy to share more details or
provide an exclusive early access.

Thanks,
[Your name]"

Day 3-5: Network Preparation
---------------------------
Tasks:
□ List all personal connections (LinkedIn, Twitter, email)
□ Create spreadsheet of 100+ contacts to notify
□ Draft launch announcement messages (3 versions)
□ Prepare email sequence (pre-launch, launch day, follow-up)
□ Set up Slack/Discord alerts for PH discussions

Day 6-7: Community Building
---------------------------
Tasks:
□ Join Product Hunt community (Slack, Discord, subreddit)
□ Engage with 5+ other launches (thoughtful comments)
□ Start following relevant makers and potential users
□ Contribute value to discussions (no spam)


WEEK 2: ASSETS PREPARATION
===========================

Day 8-9: Visual Assets
----------------------
Tasks:
□ Create 5-10 high-quality product screenshots
□ Record 60-90 second product video (screen + voiceover)
□ Create 3-5 GIF demonstrations of key features
□ Design launcher card graphic (848x400px)
□ Prepare team/company photo for maker profile

Asset Specifications:
- Screenshots: 1280x800px minimum, PNG format
- Video: MP4, under 500MB, 1080p preferred
- GIFs: 480px width, under 2MB each
- Launcher card: JPG or PNG, under 500KB

Day 10-11: Written Content
--------------------------
Tasks:
□ Write 3 tagline options (under 60 characters each)
□ Draft maker's comment (200-400 words, authentic story)
□ Prepare feature highlights (3-5 bullet points)
□ Write first comment response templates
□ Create FAQ document for common questions

Day 12-14: PR & Distribution
----------------------------
Tasks:
□ Create press kit (Zip including all assets + description)
□ Draft 3 different outreach emails for journalists
□ Prepare social media posts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook)
□ Schedule posts with Buffer/Hootsuite
□ Set up UTM parameters for all tracking links


WEEK 3: CONTENT OPTIMIZATION
============================

Day 15-16: Listing Polish
-------------------------
Tasks:
□ Finalize tagline (A/B test with friends if unsure)
□ Write 3 versions of product description
□ Select best screenshots and arrange in narrative order
□ Add video thumbnail that captures attention
□ Include pricing info (free, freemium, paid)

Day 17-18: Story Crafting
------------------------
Tasks:
□ Write origin story (why you built this)
□ Identify unique angle/insight for coverage
□ Create "discussion starters" for comments
□ Prepare comparison to alternatives (without naming)
□ Identify press-worthy announcements

Day 19-21: Testing & Feedback
----------------------------
Tasks:
□ Test all links and tracking
□ Preview listing on staging
□ Get 3-5 trusted users to review listing
□ Incorporate feedback on clarity
□ Practice pitch in under 2 minutes


WEEK 4: FINAL COUNTDOWN
=======================

Day 22-23: Soft Launch (D-7 to D-5)
-----------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Post teaser on social media
□ Email waitlist subscribers
□ Send private beta to select users
□ Gather initial testimonials
□ Refine based on early feedback

Day 24-25: Network Activation (D-4 to D-3)
------------------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Send personalized DMs to close contacts
□ Post in relevant communities (not spam)
□ Share with industry groups on LinkedIn
□ Reach out to micro-influencers
□ Confirm hunter is ready

Day 26-27: Final Preparation (D-2 to D-1)
----------------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Finalize all assets
□ Confirm hunter submission time
□ Prepare launch day schedule
□ Set up monitoring dashboard
□ Charge laptop and ensure good internet

Day 28: Launch Day
------------------
Tasks:
□ Hunter posts at optimal time (midnight UTC if targeting US)
□ Post maker comment within first 5 minutes
□ Set up all-day monitoring
□ Execute social media schedule
□ Reply to every comment within 15 minutes
```


### 3. Maker Profile Optimization

```
MAKER PROFILE CHECKLIST
=======================

Profile Essentials:
□ Professional headshot (not logo)
□ Full name and role
□ Company/team name
□ Location (optional but adds authenticity)
□ Bio (2-3 sentences, includes "Founder of [X]")

Bio Optimization:
- First sentence: What you do/worth knowing
- Second sentence: What you recently launched/built
- Third sentence: Interesting fact or CTA

Good Example:
"Building the future of remote work tools. Founder of
TeamFlow (Y Combinator W21). Previously PM at Stripe.
Love: coffee, dogs, fixing broken products."

Profile Credibility Boosters:
□ Previous successful launches
□ Portfolio of past projects
□ Links to social profiles
□ Team members with completed profiles
□ Verification badges (if available)

Engagement Strategy:
□ Comment on 3+ other launches daily before your launch
□ Reply thoughtfully to comments on your launch
□ Share behind-the-scenes content
□ Update profile after successful launch
```

```
-----------------------------------------

16:00 - Post in PH community
17:00 - Personal outreach to influencers
18:00 - Final daily push
19:00 - Evening engagement
20:00 - Thank you posts begin
21:00 - Day 1 wrap-up


HOURLY ACTION ITEMS
====================

Hour 1 (Launch):
□ Verify listing is live
□ Post maker comment immediately
□ Add product video
□ Pin best screenshot first

Hours 2-4:
□ Reply to all comments within 15 minutes
□ Share on Twitter with screenshot
□ Email top 50 contacts personally
□ Post in 2-3 relevant communities

Hours 5-8:
□ Continue engagement
□ Share second content piece
□ Thank big supporters personally
□ Update on any press mentions

Hours 9-12:
□ Mid-launch momentum push
□ Share new testimonial if available
□ Final social distribution
□ Begin thank you content


REAL-TIME MONITORING METRICS
==============================

Track Every 2 Hours:
- Total upvotes
- Total comments
- Website traffic (GA)
- Signups/trials
- New Twitter followers
- Hacker News traffic (if cross-posted)

Alerts to Set:
- Website traffic spike > 50% in 1 hour
- Signup rate > normal by 3x
- Any press/media mentions
- Social mentions spike
```


### 5. Post-Launch Strategy

```
POST-LAUNCH STRATEGY (Days 1-7)
===============================

DAY 1: IMMEDIATE FOLLOW-UP
---------------------------

Morning:
□ Analyze overnight metrics
□ Identify top traffic sources
□ Thank everyone who upvoted (personalized)
□ Post update on PH about launch success

Afternoon:
□ Reach out to all new signups personally
□ Collect initial feedback
□ Identify quick wins (feature requests)
□ Begin case study documentation

Evening:
□ Write launch retrospective
□ Plan next 48-hour outreach
□ Prepare follow-up content


DAY 2-3: CAPITALIZE ON MOMENTUM
--------------------------------

Tasks:
□ Reach out to journalists who cover your category
□ Submit to similar directories (AlternativeTo, etc.)
□ Update website with PH badge if top 5
□ Send thank you email to entire list
□ Post launch results on LinkedIn

Press Outreach Template:
"Hi [Journalist],

[Product name] just launched on Product Hunt and became
[#X] product of the day. We solve [problem] for [audience].

[One sentence on why it's interesting/different]

Would you be interested in covering this? Happy to share more
info or set up a demo.

Thanks,
[Your name]"


DAY 4-7: SUSTAIN GROWTH
-------------------------

Tasks:
□ Follow up with warm leads
□ Convert trial users to paid
□ Gather testimonials from new users
□ Update product with PH feedback
□ Plan next launch/milestone announcement

Metrics to Track:
- New signups from PH (track with UTM)
- Conversion rate of PH traffic
- Retention of PH acquired users
- Press coverage generated
- Social mentions continuing


WEEK 2+: LONG-TERM VALUE
-------------------------

If Successful (>300 upvotes, top 5):
□ Set up automated PH listing update
□ Plan quarterly PH presence
□ Build PH community relationships
□ Consider PH advertising for future launches

If Less Successful:
□ Analyze what went wrong
□ Document learnings
□ Plan re-launch strategy
□ Consider different hunter/angle
□ Don't give up - many products succeed on 2nd attempt


CASE STUDY: LESSONS FROM SUCCESSFUL LAUNCHES
=============================================

What Worked:
1. Posting at midnight UTC (timezone advantage)
2. Having hunter with 1000+ followers
3. Strong 60-second video demo
4. Personal outreach to 100+ people
5. Responding to every comment
6. Having a unique angle/insight

What Failed:
1. Posting without video
2. Generic tagline
3. No pre-launch audience building
4. Ignoring community engagement
5. Poor screenshot quality
6. No follow-up plan


SUCCESS METRICS
================

Primary:
- Upvotes: 300+ (top 5 threshold varies by day)
- Comments: 50+ (engagement indicator)
- Website traffic: 1000+ unique visitors
- Signups: 100+ new accounts

Secondary:
- Press mentions: 3+ articles
- Social followers: +200
- Email list growth: +500
- Community members: +50

Quality:
- Trial to paid conversion: >5%
- 30-day retention: >40%
- NPS from PH users: >50
```


### 6. Common Pitfalls to Avoid

```
LAUNCH MISTAKES TO AVOID
=========================

Pre-Launch:
✗ Waiting until last week to find hunter
✗ No community engagement before launch
✗ Generic or unclear product description
✗ Low-quality screenshots or no video
✗ Posting without UTM tracking
✗ Not preparing network

Launch Day:
✗ Posting without maker comment ready
✗ Ignoring comments for hours
✗ Only posting once and disappearing
✗ Spamming communities
✗ Defensive responses to criticism
✗ No monitoring/slow responses

Post-Launch:
✗ No follow-up with leads
✗ Ignoring PH feedback
✗ Celebrating without executing
✗ Not tracking PH-attributed metrics
✗ Missing press opportunities
✗ No plan for next launch


PRO TIPS FROM EXPERIENCED MAKERS
=================================

1. "The hunter matters more than the timing" - Always vet hunters

2. "Video is non-negotiable" - Motion captures attention in feed

3. "First hour sets the trajectory" - Pre-warm your network

4. "Respond to every comment" - Engagement signals quality

5. "Have something new to share" - Exclusive info for PH users

6. "Cross-post to HN, Reddit, Twitter" - Multiply reach

7. "Thank people personally" - Builds lasting relationships

8. "Launch day is just day 1" - Plan 30 days of follow-up
```

# Marketing Playbooks

## 1. Zero to First 100 Customers

### 1.1 Playbook Overview

```
Playbook: Zero to First 100 Customers
======================================

Objective: Acquire first 100 paying customers
Timeline: 90 days
Target: [Define your customer]

PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-15)
================================

Week 1: Product-Market Fit Validation
--------------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Define MVP features
□ Create waitlist/interest form
□ Identify 20 potential customers to interview
□ Conduct 10 customer interviews
□ Document learnings

Deliverables:
- Refined value proposition
- Customer interview notes
- Product feedback summary

Week 2: Positioning and Messaging
---------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Finalize brand positioning
□ Create messaging hierarchy
□ Develop 3 core marketing messages
□ Prepare pitch deck (if B2B)

Deliverables:
- Positioning statement
- Key messages
- Pitch deck

Week 3: Channel Selection
--------------------------
Tasks:
□ List 10 potential acquisition channels
□ Score channels (cost, time, fit)
□ Select top 3 channels to test
□ Set up tracking for each channel

Deliverables:
- Channel selection matrix
- Tracking setup

Week 4: Minimum Viable Assets
------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Create landing page (3-5 sections)
□ Set up email capture
□ Prepare 1 lead magnet
□ Set up Google Analytics

Deliverables:
- Live landing page
- Email capture system
- Tracking dashboard


PHASE 2: ACQUISITION (Days 16-60)
===================================

Week 5-6: Channel Testing
-------------------------
Tasks:
□ Test Channel 1 (allocate 33% of effort)
  - Run small campaigns
  - Collect data
  
□ Test Channel 2 (allocate 33% of effort)
  - Run small campaigns
  - Collect data
  
□ Test Channel 3 (allocate 33% of effort)
  - Run small campaigns
  - Collect data

Deliverables:
- Channel performance data
- Cost per lead for each channel

Week 7-8: Channel Optimization
-------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Double down on best-performing channel
□ Kill underperforming channels
□ Create content for winning channel
□ Set up automated email sequences

Deliverables:
- Optimized channel strategy
- Email sequences (3-5 emails)

Week 9-10: Volume Scaling
-------------------------
Tasks:
□ Increase budget on winning channel
□ Create 10x more content/assets
□ Launch referral program (if applicable)
□ Optimize conversion funnel

Deliverables:
- Scaled acquisition engine
- Conversion optimization improvements


PHASE 3: OPTIMIZATION (Days 61-90)
===================================

Week 11-12: Retention Focus
----------------------------
Tasks:
□ Analyze early customer feedback
□ Implement customer success process
□ Create onboarding sequence
□ Collect testimonials

Deliverables:
- Onboarding flow
- 3-5 customer testimonials

Week 13: Milestone Review
-------------------------
Tasks:
□ Count total customers
□ Calculate metrics (CAC, LTV, conversion)
□ Identify bottlenecks
□ Plan next 90 days

Deliverables:
- Metrics dashboard
- Next quarter plan


SUCCESS METRICS:
----------------
- Customers: 100
- CAC: < $[X]
- Conversion Rate: > [X%]
- NPS: > 50
```

## 2. Product Hunt Launch

### 2.1 Playbook Overview

```
Playbook: Product Hunt Launch
==============================

Objective: Achieve top 5 product of the day
Timeline: 4 weeks before + launch day + 1 week after
Key Platform: Product Hunt

PREPARATION (Weeks 1-3)
========================

Week 1: Hunt Preparation
-------------------------
Tasks:
□ Apply for Hunter (if you don't have one)
□ Identify potential hunters
□ Research similar past launches
□ Prepare assets

Assets Needed:
- Product screenshots (5-10)
- Product video (60-90 seconds)
- GIF demonstrations
- Press kit

Week 2: Community Building
--------------------------
Tasks:
□ Engage on Product Hunt daily
□ Comment on other launches
□ Build relationships with makers
□ Join PH communities (Slack, Discord)

Week 3: Launch Assets
--------------------
Tasks:
□ Finalize PH listing content
□ Write compelling tagline
□ Prepare maker's comment
□ Set up tracking (UTM parameters)
□ Prepare social media posts

Week 4: Pre-Launch (Days -7 to 0)
---------------------------------
Tasks:
□ Tease upcoming launch (D-7)
□ Email waitlist (D-5)
□ Final social push (D-3)
□ Hunter notifies (D-1)
□ Final checklist (D-Day)

LAUNCH DAY (Day 0)
==================

Timeline:
00:00 - Hunter posts (if using timezone advantage)
Morning - Monitor comments
Throughout - Reply to all comments
Throughout - Share on social
Throughout - Email list
12:00 - Post in maker communities
18:00 - Final push

Tasks:
□ Post as maker with intro comment
□ Reply to all comments within 15 minutes
□ Share on Twitter every 2 hours
□ Email list (morning and evening)
□ Post in relevant communities
□ DM to personal network

Key Metrics to Monitor:
- Views
- Upvotes
- Comments
- New visitors
- Signups/trials

POST-LAUNCH (Days 1-7)
=======================

Day 1-2: Follow-up
-------------------
Tasks:
□ Thank everyone who upvoted
□ Follow up on leads
□ Write THANK YOU post
□ Update about next features

Day 3-7: Capitalize
-------------------
Tasks:
□ Collect testimonials
□ Reach out to journalists
□ Prepare case study
□ Plan next growth phase
```

## 3. Growth Experiments

### 3.1 Playbook Overview

```
Playbook: Growth Experiments Framework
======================================

Objective: Run continuous growth experiments
Framework: Build-Measure-Learn
Timeline: Ongoing sprints

EXPERIMENT SPRINT STRUCTURE
============================

Sprint Length: 2 weeks

Day 1: Ideation
----------------
Tasks:
□ Review metrics and identify opportunities
□ Brainstorm 10+ experiment ideas
□ Score ideas (impact, confidence, ease)
□ Select top 3 experiments

Experiment Score Card:
Idea: [Name]
Impact (1-10): [X]
Confidence (1-10): [X]
Ease (1-10): [X]
Score (I × C × E): [X]

Day 2-3: Planning
------------------
For top experiment:
□ Write hypothesis
□ Define success metrics
□ Create variant
□ Set up tracking
□ Determine sample size

Hypothesis Template:
"If we [change],
then [metric] will [increase/decrease] by [%],
because [reason/belief]."

Days 4-13: Running
-------------------
Tasks:
□ Launch experiment
□ Monitor for issues
□ Ensure statistical validity
□ Don't peek at results early

Day 14: Analysis
-----------------
Tasks:
□ Pull results
□ Calculate statistical significance
□ Document learnings
□ Share with team
□ Decide: Implement/Kill/Iterate

Results Template:
Experiment: [Name]
Duration: [X] days
Sample Size: [X] per variant
Result: [Winner] / [Statistically Significant: Yes/No]
Primary Metric Change: [+/-X%]
Learning: [What we learned]


GROWTH EXPERIMENT IDEAS
=========================

ACQUISITION EXPERIMENTS
-----------------------
1. Homepage headline test
2. Landing page CTA test
3. Ad copy rotation
4. New traffic source test
5. SEO title optimization
6. Social proof placement

ACTIVATION EXPERIMENTS
-----------------------
1. Onboarding flow test
2. First-use celebration
3. Feature tour variation
4. Progress indicator addition
5. Tooltip timing test
6. Empty state design

RETENTION EXPERIMENTS
---------------------
1. Email frequency test
2. Push notification timing
3. Re-engagement campaign
4. Loyalty program test
5. Community feature launch
6. Milestone celebration

REFERRAL EXPERIMENTS
--------------------
1. Referral email timing
2. Incentive level test
3. Share button placement
4. Social proof framing
5. Referral page design
6. Micro-rewards test


COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID
==========================

✗ Testing too many things at once
✗ Not running to statistical significance
✗ Ignoring secondary metrics
✗ Testing minor changes with big impact expectation
✗ Not documenting learnings
✗ Confirmation bias in analysis
```

## 4. Quick Wins Playbook

### 4.1 Playbook Overview

```
Playbook: 30-Day Marketing Quick Wins
=====================================

Objective: Implement 10 high-impact, low-effort marketing tactics
Timeline: 30 days (3 tactics per week)

WEEK 1: CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION
==============================

Day 1-2: Landing Page Audit
----------------------------
Tactics:
□ Add clear value proposition above fold
□ Test CTA button color
□ Add trust badges

Day 3-4: Form Optimization
---------------------------
Tactics:
□ Reduce form fields to essentials
□ Add inline validation
□ Test button copy

Day 5-7: Social Proof
----------------------
Tactics:
□ Add testimonials to homepage
□ Add logo bar to landing page
□ Create case study page


WEEK 2: CONTENT QUICK WINS
===========================

Day 8-9: Blog Optimization
---------------------------
Tactics:
□ Add internal links to 5 old posts
□ Update meta descriptions
□ Add CTAs to posts

Day 10-11: Content Repurposing
-------------------------------
Tactics:
□ Turn blog post into social posts (5)
□ Turn webinar into blog post
□ Create quote graphics from content

Day 12-14: Email List
----------------------
Tactics:
□ Add popup to website
□ Create simple lead magnet
□ Set up welcome email


WEEK 3: SEO IMPROVEMENTS
=========================

Day 15-16: Technical SEO
------------------------
Tactics:
□ Submit sitemap to Google
□ Fix broken links
□ Optimize images

Day 17-18: Content SEO
-----------------------
Tactics:
□ Update 3 old posts with new info
□ Add FAQ sections to posts
□ Improve internal linking

Day 19-21: Local SEO (if applicable)
------------------------------------
Tactics:
□ Set up Google Business Profile
□ Add location pages
□ Get reviews


WEEK 4: SOCIAL & REFERRAL
===========================

Day 22-23: LinkedIn Optimization
---------------------------------
Tactics:
□ Update profile to completion
□ Add keywords to headline
□ Pin best-performing content

Day 24-25: Referral Program
---------------------------
Tactics:
□ Create referral email template
□ Add "Share" buttons to product
□ Set up referral tracking

Day 26-30: Analytics & Documentation
-------------------------------------
Tactics:
□ Set up conversion tracking
□ Document what worked
□ Plan Month 2 improvements


EXPECTED OUTCOMES
==================

If executed well:
- Conversion rate: +10-20%
- Email list: +100-500 subscribers
- SEO traffic: +5-15%
- Referral traffic: +10-20%
```

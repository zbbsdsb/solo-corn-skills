Strategic Decision

Business model and competitive strategy frameworks.

What It Does

Strategic Decision provides frameworks and mental models for making high-stakes business decisions. From business model design to competitive positioning, it helps you think through strategic choices systematically, reducing cognitive bias and increasing decision quality.

Core Capabilities

  • Business model design and validation frameworks
  • Competitive analysis and positioning strategies
  • Decision-making under uncertainty with optionality thinking
  • Strategic planning and roadmap prioritization

Quick Facts

Best For
Business model design, competitive strategy, major decisions
Integrates With
Research Integration, Landing, Product Builder
Complexity
Advanced
Output
Strategy documents, decision memos, business models

Workflow

01

Frame Decision

Define the decision scope, stakeholders, and success criteria clearly.

02

Gather Intelligence

Research market dynamics, competitors, and internal capabilities.

03

Generate Options

Use mental models to develop multiple strategic alternatives.

04

Commit & Execute

Make the decision, document rationale, and align team on execution.

When To Use

Business Model Design

Design and validate your revenue model, pricing strategy, and value capture mechanisms.

Competitive Response

Analyze competitor moves and develop strategic responses that leverage your unique advantages.

Major Investment

Evaluate high-stakes investments like new markets, partnerships, or product lines.

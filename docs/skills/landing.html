Validation

Landing

First-principles idea validation before you build.

What It Does

Landing helps you validate your ideas before investing time and resources in development. Using first-principles thinking, it guides you through problem validation, solution testing, and market demand verification to ensure you're building something people actually want.

Core Capabilities

  • Problem validation through customer discovery interviews
  • Solution hypothesis testing with landing page experiments
  • Market demand measurement and pre-launch validation
  • Risk assessment and go/no-go decision frameworks

Quick Facts

Best For
Idea validation, pre-launch testing, pivot decisions
Integrates With
Product Builder, Marketing Growth, Research Integration
Complexity
Intermediate
Output
Validation reports, landing pages, customer insights

Workflow

01

Deconstruct Problem

Break down your assumed problem into fundamental truths and validate with real users.

02

Test Solution Fit

Create minimum viable tests to validate your solution hypothesis before building.

03

Measure Demand

Use landing pages, waitlists, and pre-sales to quantify actual market interest.

04

Decide & Pivot

Based on data, decide to proceed, pivot, or abandon with clear criteria.

When To Use

New Idea

Before writing any code, validate that your idea solves a real problem for real people.

Feature Addition

Test demand for new features before adding complexity to your existing product.

Pivot Evaluation

Systematically evaluate whether a pivot direction has sufficient market validation.

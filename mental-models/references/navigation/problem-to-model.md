## Decision Tree: Which Model to Use?

```
Are you trying to UNDERSTAND why something happened?
├── Likely cause seems obvious? → [Hanlon's Razor](../decision-reasoning/hanlons-razor.md)
├── Attributing success? → [Survivorship Bias Awareness](../decision-reasoning/survivorship-bias-awareness.md)
├── Seeing unintended consequences? → [Second-Order Thinking](../decision-reasoning/second-order-thinking.md)
└── Complex system behavior? → [Feedback Loops](../systems-complexity/feedback-loops.md)

Are you trying to DECIDE what to do?
├── Multiple options? → [Two Boxes Thinking](../execution-learning/two-boxes-thinking.md)
├── High uncertainty? → [Optionality](../execution-learning/optionality.md), [Margins of Safety](../execution-learning/margins-of-safety.md)
├── Reversing failure? → [Inversion](../decision-reasoning/inversion.md)
└── Fast-moving situation? → [OODA Loop](../execution-learning/ooda-loop.md)

Are you trying to PREDICT what will happen?
├── Probabilistic outcome? → [Bayesian Thinking](../decision-reasoning/bayesian-thinking.md)
├── Long-term trend? → [Compound Growth](../execution-learning/compound-growth.md)
├── Phase transition? → [Tipping Points](../systems-complexity/tipping-points.md)
└── Complex system? → [Chaos Theory Basics](../systems-complexity/chaos-theory-basics.md)

Are you trying to INFLUENCE behavior?
├── Designing incentives? → [Incentive Structures](../psychology-behavior/incentive-structures.md)
├── Leveraging social proof? → [Social Proof](../psychology-behavior/social-proof.md)
├── Creating urgency? → [Scarcity Effect](../psychology-behavior/scarcity-effect.md)
└── Building commitment? → [Commitment Consistency](../psychology-behavior/commitment-consistency.md)

Are you trying to CHANGE a system?
├── Finding where to intervene? → [Leverage Points](../systems-complexity/leverage-points.md)
├── Balancing competing forces? → [Balancing Loops](../systems-complexity/balancing-loops.md)
├── Accelerating growth? → [Reinforcement Loops](../systems-complexity/reinforcement-loops.md)
└── Understanding emergence? → [Emergence](../systems-complexity/emergence.md)
```

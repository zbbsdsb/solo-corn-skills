# Idea Validation Resources

## 1. Market Analysis Framework

### 1.1 Market Size Assessment

**Steps:**
1. Define target market segments
2. Estimate addressable market size
3. Analyze market growth rate
4. Identify market trends

**Key Questions:**
- Who are the target users?
- What is the market size (TAM, SAM, SOM)?
- What is the growth trajectory?
- What macro trends are affecting this market?

**Output Template:**
```
Market Analysis Summary
├── Target Market: [Description]
├── TAM: [Total Addressable Market]
├── SAM: [Serviceable Addressable Market]
├── SOM: [Serviceable Obtainable Market]
├── Growth Rate: [X% annually]
└── Key Trends: [Trend 1, Trend 2, Trend 3]
```

### 1.2 Competitor Analysis

**Competitor Types:**
- Direct competitors
- Indirect competitors
- Potential entrants

**Analysis Dimensions:**
- Product features
- Pricing models
- Customer experience
- Market positioning
- Strengths and weaknesses

**Competitor Scorecard:**
| Competitor | Features | Pricing | UX | Strengths | Weaknesses |

### 1.3 Feasibility Assessment

**Technical Feasibility:**
- Required technology stack
- Development complexity
- Resource requirements
- Timeline estimate

**Business Feasibility:**
- Revenue potential
- Cost structure
- Break-even analysis
- Funding requirements

**Feasibility Checklist:**
- [ ] Core features can be built with available resources
- [ ] Timeline is reasonable (under 6 months for MVP)
- [ ] Market demand exists
- [ ] Profitability is achievable
- [ ] Team has necessary skills or can acquire them

## 2. Idea Scoring System

### Scoring Criteria (1-5 scale)

| Criterion | Description | Weight |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Market Size | Addressable market opportunity | 25% |
| Market Growth | Growth rate and trends | 15% |
| Competition | Competitive landscape | 15% |
| Technical Feasibility | Implementation difficulty | 15% |
| Business Viability | Revenue potential | 20% |
| Team Fit | Team skills match | 10% |

### Score Interpretation

| Score Range | Assessment | Recommendation |
|-------------|------------|----------------|
| 4.0-5.0 | Excellent | Proceed with high confidence |
| 3.0-3.9 | Good | Proceed with caution |
| 2.0-2.9 | Fair | Reconsider or pivot |
| <2.0 | Poor | Abandon or significantly revise |

## 3. Validation Questions

### Customer Validation
- Who has this problem?
- How are they currently solving it?
- Would they pay for a solution?
- What would they be willing to pay?

### Problem Validation
- Is this a real pain point?
- How frequently does the problem occur?
- What is the impact of the problem?
- Are people actively searching for solutions?

### Solution Validation
- Does this solution solve the problem?
- Is it better than existing alternatives?
- Is it easy to understand and use?
- Can it be delivered at scale?

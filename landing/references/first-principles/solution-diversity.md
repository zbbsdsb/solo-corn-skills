## SCAMPER: Systematic Solution Variation

### The Seven Transformation Techniques

**S - Substitute**: What or who else? What other ingredients, materials, processes, people, approaches?

**C - Combine**: What can be combined? What if we merge this with something else?

**A - Adapt**: How else might this be done? What else is like this? What other ideas could I borrow?

**M - Modify**: What can we change? Bigger? Smaller? Higher? Lower? Faster? Slower?

**P - Put to other uses**: What else can this be used for? New markets? New contexts?

**E - Eliminate**: What if we remove elements? Simplify? Create an entirely "less is more" version?

**R - Reverse**: What if we flip things around? Inside out? Back to front? Opposite approach?

## Final Solution Diversity Checklist

Before finalizing your approach:

- [ ] Have I generated at least 5 distinct solutions?
- [ ] Have I explored solutions from other domains?
- [ ] Have I tried the "opposite" approach?
- [ ] Have I questioned every assumption in my favorite solution?
- [ ] Have I generated solutions with different cost/time/impact tradeoffs?
- [ ] Have I explored "do nothing" as a valid option?
- [ ] Have I considered solutions that require breaking conventional rules?
- [ ] Have I challenged myself to think at the first principles level?

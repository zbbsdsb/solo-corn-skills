## Template Usage Instructions
This template is based on Connector Skill's cognitive science design principles:
- **Cognitive Load Management**: Layered information disclosure
- **Generation Effect**: Active filling instead of passive reading
- **Transfer Learning**: Force search for cross-domain connections
- **Metacognition**: Self-assessment and reflection

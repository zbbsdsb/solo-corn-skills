## Design Evaluation Framework
- Functionality: Solves the problem?
- Usability: Easy to use?
- Aesthetics: Looks good?
- Innovation: Has new ideas?
- Sustainability: Suitable long-term?

## Design Learning Principles
Based on cognitive science:
- **Generation Effect**: Design hands-on instead of just looking
- **Situated Learning**: Learn in real projects
- **Desirable Difficulties**: Challenge your aesthetic comfort zone
